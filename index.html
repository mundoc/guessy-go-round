How to Play

Setup

  1. Enter names for both teams and choose how many points are needed to win.
  2. Pick which word categories you want in the game.
  3. Sit in a circle alternating teams — Team A, Team B, Team A, Team B, and so on.
  4. If there's an odd number of players, one person plays as the Joker: they guess for both teams and never hold the phone.

Each Round

  1. One player holds the phone — only they can see the word on screen.
  2. Give clues to your team without saying the word itself.
  3. You can tap Change Word as many times as you want — even mid-description if your team isn't getting it.
  4. The timer runs secretly — it goes off at a random time!
  5. Once your team guesses the word, pass the phone to the next person clockwise.

Scoring

  1. After each round, use the + / − buttons to update the scores.
  2. First team to reach the points goal wins! 🏆
